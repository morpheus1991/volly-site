Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 9, 2026

Overview

Volly is a macOS menu bar app for audio volume control. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data through the app. Volly runs entirely on your Mac and does not send any data to external servers. Specifically:

  • No analytics or tracking
  • No audio data is recorded or transmitted
  • No usage data is collected
  • No cookies (the app has no network features)

Payment Data

Purchases are processed by Paddle.com, our Merchant of Record. Paddle collects your payment information (name, email, payment details) to process transactions. Please refer to Paddle's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle your data.

License Verification

Volly may contact Paddle's servers to verify your license key. This transmits only the license key itself — no other personal information.

Website

This website (volly.space) is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect basic server logs (IP addresses, browser info). See GitHub's Privacy Statement.

Third-Party Services

We use Google Fonts on this website, which may collect basic usage data per Google's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

Volly is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

We may update this policy as needed. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
